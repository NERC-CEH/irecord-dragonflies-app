Registration

Registering with iRecord is quick and easy and makes submitting biological records much easier. It provides you with a means of viewing all the records you have submitted and accessing feedback on them, as well as allowing you to view others’ records. You can also download all of your records here.

It is important that you register with iRecord before submitting mammal records through this app, as this is the database where all of The Mammal Society’s records are stored for use in atlases and ultimately mammal conservation. Records that are available on iRecord are also much more likely to be used by other organisations interested in using them for conservation and research (such as Environmental Local Record Centres).
